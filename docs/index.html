How to Create Art

Click to Create Drops

Click anywhere on the canvas to create a new drop. Each drop will use a randomly selected color from beautiful themed palettes.

Dynamic Color Palettes

Enjoy 15 carefully curated color palettes including Sunset, Ocean, Traditional Japanese, Turkish Ebru, and more artistic themes.

Watch the Magic

Each new drop interacts with existing ones, creating beautiful marbling effects inspired by traditional suminagashi techniques.

Save Your Art

Use the "Save Screenshot" button to download your creation as a PNG image to your device.
